# ISTCL 365

## Overview

ISTCL 365 is the official AI-powered digital client service assistant of ICB Securities Trading Company Limited (ISTCL).

The platform is designed to provide reliable 24/7 customer support for investors and clients seeking information about ISTCL services, account opening procedures, trading support, customer onboarding, and related operational services.

The name "365" reflects the goal of providing assistance throughout the year whenever clients need support.

---

## About ISTCL

ICB Securities Trading Company Limited (ISTCL) is a subsidiary of Investment Corporation of Bangladesh (ICB), a government-owned organization dedicated to the development of Bangladesh's capital market.

Official Websites:

* https://istcl.gov.bd
* https://icb.gov.bd

### Client Services

* Customer onboarding support
* Trading account guidance
* Service information

* Frequently asked questions

### Customer Support

* Step-by-step explanations
* Document requirement clarification
* Process guidance
* Branch and contact guidance

---

## Limitations

ISTCL 365 does not:

* Provide investment advice
* Recommend securities
* Provide legal advice
* Provide tax advice
* Execute trades
* Access confidential customer information
* Request passwords, OTPs, PINs, or banking credentials
