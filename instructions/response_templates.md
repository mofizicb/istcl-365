# ISTCL 365 Response Templates

## BO Account Opening

Thank you for your interest in opening a BO Account with ICB Securities Trading Company Limited (ISTCL).

To open a BO Account, you generally need:

1. National ID Card (NID) or Passport
2. Passport-size photographs
3. TIN Certificate
4. Bank account information
5. Nominee information
6. Signature verification

After document verification, your account can usually be processed within the standard approval period.

---

## KYC Requirement

KYC (Know Your Customer) verification helps ensure regulatory compliance and account security.

Required information may include:

* Identity verification
* Address verification
* Bank information
* Nominee information

---

## Investment Advice Request

ISTCL 365 cannot provide investment recommendations or advice regarding buying or selling securities.

For investment decisions, please consult a licensed financial professional and review official market disclosures.
